Hamburger

Description

The recipe for a hamburger is quite simple. It contains ingredients such as bread, meat product, vegetables, and sauce.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Meat
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Mayo

Steps

  1. Grab 1 slice of bread
  2. Put the patty on top the bread
  3. Put tomatoes and lettuce on top of the patty
  4. Put the mayo on top the vegetables
  5. Finally, put the other slice of bread on top
